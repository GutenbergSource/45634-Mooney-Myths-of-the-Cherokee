A large collection of Cherokee myths that originally appeared in the BAE (Bureau of American Ethnology) publication, vol. 19.

Although old (in PGDP terms), the BAE Formatting still apply. In particular, carefully take care of the various types of primes and apostrophes being used in this text.

With over 600 pages of tightly set text and many uncommon accents, the work will not always be easy, but this is certainly an interesting and worthwhile text.

Have fun!
